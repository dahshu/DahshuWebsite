Short Courses

Our short courses are uniquely designed to offer intensive, focused training in specific areas of data science, providing attendees with both in-depth knowledge and practical, hands-on experience. These sessions are distinct from the general symposium registration and require separate enrollment.

Course Schedule and Selection:

This year, we are excited to present a structured format allowing attendees to maximize learning opportunities by attending one course in the morning and another in the afternoon. 


The offerings include:

Morning Sessions:

  • Overview of Deep Learning 

  • Single Cell and Spatial Informatics 


Afternoon Sessions:

  • Generative AI 

  • Overview of Clinical Trial Design and Current Trends
