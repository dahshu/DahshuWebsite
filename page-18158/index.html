CALL FOR POSTER PRESENTATIONS

Abstract submission is now open for poster presentations at the 2025 DahShu Data Science Symposium. We invite submissions from students, trainees, and professionals to showcase their work related to AI, data science, and statistics.

Prizes will be awarded for outstanding poster presentations that are most relevant to the theme of the symposium. Due to space constraints, a limited number of submissions will be accepted. To apply, please submit a title and abstract by October 3, 2025. The size of the poster can be at most 36 inches tall and 48 inches wide.

The presenting author must register the conference and present in-person.
