ABOUT

We cordially invite you to join the DahShu Data Science Symposium. As a non-profit organization, DahShu is dedicated to advancing research and education in data science. Our global membership surpasses 6,000 individuals, and our monthly virtual journal club consistently attracts over 100 attendees. We are committed to expanding our services to better serve our community.

This year, we invited speakers, including keynote speakers and invited speakers, and will have short courses, scientific sessions, and poster presentations.
