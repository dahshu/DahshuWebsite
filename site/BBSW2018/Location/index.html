LOCATION

Crowne Plaza Foster City

1221 Chess Drive

Foster City, CA 94404


Located at the heart of the biotech region, and the leading edge of the Silicon Valley, 15 minutes from San Francisco International Airport and 30 minutes from downtown San Francisco.


Accommodations

Conference attendees can get a room discount by clicking here


Complimentary parking on site.

HOW TO GET HERE

From the South (San Jose)

Take Highway US-101 North, then take CA-92 E to Metro Center Blvd in Foster City. Next, take exit 14B from CA-92 E and continue on Metro Center Blvd. Finally, take Vintage Park Dr to Chess Dr.

From the North (San Francisco)

Take Highway US-101 South to Foster City and take exit 14B from CA-92 E. Next, continue on Metro Center Blvd and take Vintage Park Dr to Chess Dr.
