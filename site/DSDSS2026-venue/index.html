Helpful Tips When Visiting Campus

Recommended attire is business casual. We do recommend that you dress in layers, as temperatures in meeting rooms can vary. Comfortable shoes are also recommended for navigating campus.

Bring an umbrella, as the weather may vary during your stay.

There are water-bottle filling stations throughout campus, so bring yours! Can’t find a filling station? Ask one of your UConn hosts!

10 Things To Do While Visiting UConn Storrs

10 Things To Do While Visiting UConn Storrs

Whether you're a prospective student, a visiting family member, or just exploring the area, the University of Connecticut's Storrs campus offers a variety of attractions and activities. Here are ten must-visit spots, you can find them using our campus interactive map:
1. Spring Valley Student Farm Learn about sustainable agriculture and student-led farming initiatives at this off-campus farm.
Spring Valley Student Farm
2. The William Benton Museum of Art The Benton Museum features an impressive collection of artwork, exhibitions, and events.
The Benton Museum
3. The Ballard Institute and Museum of Puppetry Explore the fascinating world of puppetry, with exhibits and live performances.
Ballard Institute and Museum of Puppetry
4. UConn Athletics Catch a Huskies game and experience the excitement of UConn sports.
UConn Athletics
5. Downtown Storrs Enjoy restaurants, shops, and cultural events that include live music in the lively downtown area.
Downtown Storrs
6. Jorgensen Center for the Performing Arts Experience concerts, theater performances, and dance shows at UConn’s premier performing arts venue.
Jorgensen Center for the Performing Arts
7. UConn Dairy Bar Indulge in delicious ice cream made on campus with locally sourced ingredients.
UConn Dairy Bar
8. Horsebarn Hill Walk along scenic trails, visit the dairy barns, and enjoy panoramic views of the campus.
Horsebarn Hill
9. UConn Bookstore Grab Husky gear, books, and souvenirs at the UConn Bookstore.
UConn Bookstore
10. Student Union A hub for campus life, the Student Union offers a convenience store and soft seating for networking chats.
UConn Student Union
