DahShu Data Science Symposium 2025

Innovative Frontiers: AI and Data-Driven Advances in Drug Development, Precision Medicine, Healthcare


 

  October 16-18, 2025

   University of Connecticut

    Storrs | Connecticut

                           


Conference Information: The 2025 symposium will focus on how cutting-edge advancements in AI, data science, and big data analytics are transforming biomedical research, drug development, and healthcare & clinical practice, while also stimulating further development of fundamental research and fostering collaborations among academia, industry, and regulatory bodies. We will explore a broad range of topics, including AI-driven drug discovery, Automation using AI/ML to increase the efficiency and productivity of drug development, multi-omics integration, advanced predictive analytics for clinical trials, computational biology, and the use of real-world evidence in decision-making. The symposium will feature a series of scientific sessions and interactive short courses that not only showcase state-of-the-art research but also foster vibrant discussions on the opportunities and challenges of driving precision medicine forward.
