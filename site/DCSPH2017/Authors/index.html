CALL FOR POSTERS

Researchers wishing to present their research without submitting full-length papers are encouraged to submit a one page abstract to present their work in the poster sessions.

Important dates

  • Abstract submission deadline: January 27, 2017 11:59PM PT
  • Acceptance notification: February 10, 2017 11:59PM PT

Submission Instructions

  • Poster abstracts must be submitted at https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=cph2017
  • The poster abstracts should fit in ONE PAGE in A4 size (no words limit) with font size larger than 11
  • The abstract should include the following sections. You can prepare your CPH 2017 poster abstract using the template: Word Template (Click Here)
    • Background
    • Results
    • Conclusion
  • Poster abstract should include names and affiliations of all authors, email address of the corresponding author, and a statement that all co-authors concur with the contents of the poster.
  • The accepted file format is PDF (Adobe Acrobat preferred). Hardcopy submissions, unprocessed TEX or LATEX files or electronic submissions not submitted through the paper management system will be rejected without review.
  • Submitted files should be named with the last name of the first author (e.g. smith.pdf).


CALL FOR PAPERS

In addition to keynote and invited presentations, CPH2017 will feature presentations of rigorously peer-reviewed full-length papers reporting on original work. The best papers will be presented orally to the entire conference, and be invited to publish in the Journal of Computational Biology or Statistics in Biosciences after the conference. Prizes will be awarded for best presentations.


Important dates

  • Paper submissions due: October 31, 2016 11:59PM PT
  • Notification of paper acceptance: December 5, 2016 11:59PM PT
  • Meeting: February 20-22, 2017
  • Final version of the paper due: March 13, 2017 11:59PM PT
  • Papers must be submitted at https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=cph2017


Submission Instructions

  • Submitted papers are limited to twenty (20) pages (not including the cover letter).
  • Please format your paper according to instructions of Journal of Computational Biology
    (http://www.liebertpub.com/overview/journal-of-computational-biology/31/)
    or Statistics in Biosciences
    (http://www.springer.com/statistics/life+sciences%2C+medicine+%26+health/journal/12561?detailsPage=pltci_1060615)
    or Model Assisted Statistics and Applications-International journal (MASA)
    (http://www.iospress.nl/journal/model-assisted-statistics-and-applications/).
  • If figures cannot be easily resized and placed precisely in the text, then it should be clear that with appropriate modifications, the total manuscript length would be within the page limit. Color images are accepted.
  • The accepted file format is PDF (Adobe Acrobat preferred). Hardcopy submissions, unprocessed TEX or LATEX files or electronic submissions not submitted through the paper management system will be rejected without review.
  • Submitted files should be named with the last name of the first author (e.g. smith.pdf).
  • Each paper must be accompanied by a cover letter. The cover letter should be the first page of your paper submission. The cover letter must state the following:
    • The email address of the corresponding author.
    • The submitted paper contains original, unpublished results, and is not currently under consideration elsewhere.
    • All co-authors concur with the contents of the paper.
  • At least one of the authors has to present at the conference in order for the paper to be published in the journals.
  • Once in the easy chair system, please click the "new submission" button on the upper left corner to start submitting the paper.
