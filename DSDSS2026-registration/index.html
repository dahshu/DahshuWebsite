Refund & Transfer Policy:

Please note that all registrations are non-refundable. Unattended fees will support Dahshu's charitable initiatives.

We understand that plans can change, which is why we offer the flexibility to transfer both your symposium and workshop registrations. Should you be unable to attend the DahShu Data Science Symposium or the workshop you've registered for, rest assured that your registration can be transferred to another individual. To initiate a transfer, please send a detailed request to dahshu2025@gmail.com. Our team is here to help make the transition as smooth as possible.
