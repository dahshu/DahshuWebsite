Abstract Submission

We invite all attendees to contribute their research and insights by submitting an abstract for presentation. This year, we are pleased to announce the availability of several poster awards, designed to recognize outstanding contributions and innovative research in the fields of data science and medical insights. To ensure a comprehensive review of your work, please indicate the following information in your abstract submission:

Category: Indicate the primary research area of your submission from the following options:

  • Enhancing Electronic Health Records with Natural Language Processing and Large Language Models
  • Multi-Omics Approaches to Unraveling Complex Diseases
  • Advancements in Data Science for Single Cell Omics
  • The Frontier of Genomics: Data Science in Long-Read Sequencing
  • Informatics Empowering Women's Health
  • P4 (Pharmacoinformatics, Pharmacoepidemiology, System Pharmacology, and Pharmacogenomics) 
  • Artificial Intelligence in Medicine
  • Navigating the Future of Drug Discovery in Industry with Data Science
  • Designing Tomorrow’s Clinical Trials: Innovations and Challenges
  • Redefining Clinical Trial Design in the Pharmaceutical Industry
  • Others

Ensure your abstract follows the symposium's specified formatting guidelines. The provided Abstract Template includes comprehensive instructions for abstract submissions, detailing all necessary formatting requirements.

To streamline the process and accommodate varying preferences, we offer two methods for submitting your abstract:

1. During Registration: You can submit the abstract directly within the registration form. This integrated approach allows you to seamlessly register for the symposium and submit your work for consideration in one step.

2. Email Submission: If you prefer not to submit your abstract at the time of registration or if you decide to submit an abstract after registering, you have the option to email it to dahshu2024@gmail.com. To help us efficiently organize and review submissions, please format the email subject line as follows:

Abstract Submission_Category

This format will assist in the quick identification and proper categorization of your submission. Please ensure your submission is received by the specified deadline.

All abstracts must be submitted by April 12, 2024. Each submission will undergo a thorough review by our panel of experts. Decision notifications regarding oral or poster presentations will be communicated by April 27, 2024.

We look forward to your contributions and to a symposium filled with engaging discussions and groundbreaking insights. For any inquiries regarding the submission process, please don't hesitate to contact us.
